# Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN with anyone
2. Do not install apps from unknown sources
3. Avoid clicking suspicious links
4. Verify calls claiming to be from banks
5. Enable SMS/email alerts for transactions
6. Use strong passwords for banking apps
7. Do not share OTPs with anyone
8. Always check app permissions before installing
9. Immediately report fraud at https://cybercrime.gov.in
10. Call helpline 1930 in case of financial fraud
