// **Do’s**

1. Maintain a professional profile (clear bio, appropriate username)

2. Think before posting anything online

3. Use privacy settings to control who can see your content

4. Verify information before sharing (avoid fake news)

5. Be respectful and polite in comments and messages

// **Don’ts**

1. Don’t share personal information like phone number, address, or passwords

2. Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content

3. Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online arguments

4. Don’t accept friend requests from unknown people

5. Don’t overshare daily activities or location publicly
